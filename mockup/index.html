Florida Blue — Adalimumab (Humira) · Rheumatoid Arthritis

Step therapy: Required ✓
Patients must try and fail at least one conventional DMARD before adalimumab is approved:
  • Methotrexate (minimum 3 months, adequate dose)
  • Hydroxychloroquine
  • Leflunomide
  • Sulfasalazine

Benefit side: Medical (administered benefit)
Prior auth: Required · 12-month approval period
Source: Clinical Policy CP-2026-041, Page 22

Exceptions apply for documented contraindication or prior biologic treatment.
