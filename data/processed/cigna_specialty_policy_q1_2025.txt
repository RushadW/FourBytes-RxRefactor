PART A: IMMUNOLOGY AND RHEUMATOLOGY

A-1. Adalimumab (Humira and Biosimilars)

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions
Tier: Specialty
Quantity Limit: 2 syringes/month

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. Confirmed FDA-labeled diagnosis: rheumatoid arthritis, psoriatic arthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis, moderate-to-severe plaque psoriasis, or hidradenitis suppurativa.
2. Step therapy required: documentation of failure, intolerance, or contraindication to methotrexate (minimum 12 weeks for RA/PsA) or other conventional therapy as appropriate for the indication.
3. Board-certified specialist must prescribe (rheumatologist for inflammatory arthritis; gastroenterologist for IBD; dermatologist for psoriasis/HS).
4. Negative QuantiFERON-TB Gold or PPD within the past 12 months.
5. Hepatitis B surface antigen (HBsAg) and hepatitis B core antibody (HBcAb) testing required.
6. CIGNA requires use of a biosimilar adalimumab as first-line unless documented clinical rationale for reference product.

Step Therapy Required: YES
Step 1: Conventional DMARDs (MTX, SSZ, LEF) for ≥12 weeks.
Step 2: One non-TNF biologic may be required if initial DMARD failure was for RA indication.

Age Restriction: Per FDA labeling
Notes: Cigna will not authorize concurrent use of adalimumab with other biologic DMARDs or JAK inhibitors.

---

A-2. Dupilumab (Dupixent)

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions
Tier: Specialty

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. Atopic Dermatitis: Confirmed diagnosis of moderate-to-severe AD (IGA ≥3 or EASI ≥16). Must have tried and failed TWO topical therapies of different classes (corticosteroid PLUS calcineurin inhibitor) for at least 4 weeks each. Failed trial of phototherapy if available. Must be under care of a board-certified dermatologist or allergist.
2. Asthma: Confirmed moderate-to-severe asthma not controlled on medium-to-high dose ICS + LABA. Eosinophil count ≥300 cells/µL or FeNO ≥25 ppb. Must have documented at least 2 asthma exacerbations in prior 12 months.
3. CRSwNP: CT scan confirming bilateral nasal polyps. Prior treatment with intranasal corticosteroids ≥4 weeks. Prior systemic corticosteroid course.

Step Therapy Required: YES (atopic dermatitis)
Step 1: Topical corticosteroid (medium-high potency) minimum 4 weeks.
Step 2: Topical calcineurin inhibitor (tacrolimus or pimecrolimus) minimum 4 weeks.

Age Restriction: 6 months+ (AD); 12+ (asthma); 18+ (CRSwNP)

---

A-3. Etanercept (Enbrel and Biosimilars)

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions
Tier: Specialty
Quantity Limit: 4 syringes per 28 days

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. FDA-approved indication required.
2. Inadequate response to conventional DMARD therapy (minimum 3-month trial).
3. Negative TB test within 12 months.
4. Specialist prescriber.

Step Therapy Required: YES
Step 1: Methotrexate or sulfasalazine - minimum 3 months.

Notes: Biosimilar etanercept preferred over reference product. Cigna may substitute biosimilar at pharmacy level.

PART B: ENDOCRINOLOGY AND METABOLIC

B-1. Semaglutide (Ozempic, Wegovy, Rybelsus)

Coverage Status: Covered (Ozempic - diabetes covered under medical benefit when administered by healthcare provider; Wegovy - covered under obesity management benefit with additional criteria)

Tier: Specialty

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria (Wegovy - Obesity):
1. BMI ≥30 kg/m² OR BMI ≥27 kg/m² with at least one obesity-related comorbidity.
2. Failure of intensive behavioral intervention (≥6 months, documented <5% weight loss).
3. NO prior trial of GLP-1 agonist required (first-line approved for this indication).
4. Prescriber must specialize in obesity medicine OR endocrinology, OR must document completion of obesity management training.
5. No personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma.
6. Not currently on other weight management medications without Cigna medical review.

Prior Authorization Criteria (Ozempic - Type 2 Diabetes):
1. Type 2 diabetes with HbA1c ≥7.0%.
2. CIGNA does NOT require step therapy for GLP-1 agonists for type 2 diabetes when there is ASCVD, CKD, or HF comorbidity.
3. Otherwise: must have tried metformin at maximum tolerated dose for 3 months.
4. Prescriber must be managing the patient's diabetes.

Step Therapy Required: YES (diabetes, without ASCVD/CKD/HF)
Step 1: Metformin - maximum tolerated dose for 3 months.

Age Restriction: 12+ (Ozempic for T2D); 18+ (Wegovy)
Quantity Limit: 1 pen per 28 days

Notes: CIGNA covers cardiovascular risk reduction indication without step therapy when ASCVD is documented. This is more permissive than some other plans.

---

B-2. Tirzepatide (Mounjaro, Zepbound)

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions (Mounjaro - Type 2 diabetes; Zepbound - obesity)
Tier: Specialty

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria (Zepbound - Obesity):
1. BMI ≥30 OR BMI ≥27 with comorbidity.
2. Failed ≥6 months behavioral intervention.
3. Must NOT have concurrent active GLP-1 agonist therapy.
4. Endocrinologist or obesity specialist prescriber required.

Notes: Cigna treats tirzepatide similarly to semaglutide for obesity but currently requires specialist prescriber (more restrictive than semaglutide).

PART C: ONCOLOGY (Medical Benefit)

C-1. Pembrolizumab (Keytruda)

Coverage Status: Covered (Medical Benefit - IV infusion only)
Tier: Specialty Oncology

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. FDA-approved cancer indication required (NSCLC, melanoma, HNSCC, urothelial, MSI-H/dMMR, TNBC, cervical, endometrial, gastric, esophageal, biliary tract, colorectal, TMB-H, cutaneous SCC, cHL, PMBCL, MCC).
2. PD-L1 testing (TPS or CPS score) where required by FDA label for the specific indication.
3. No contraindication to immunotherapy (active autoimmune disease requiring systemic immunosuppression, active immune-mediated adverse events from prior checkpoint inhibitor).
4. Must be prescribed by or in consultation with a board-certified oncologist.
5. Dosing must conform to FDA-approved dosing schedule for the indication.

Step Therapy Required: NO (oncology indication)
Site of Care: Infusion center, hospital outpatient, or oncology office with trained staff.

---

GENERAL EXCLUSIONS AND PROVISIONS

Excluded from Coverage:
- Investigational use
- Cosmetic indications
- Off-label use without Level I evidence
- Concurrent duplicate biologic therapy (e.g., two TNF inhibitors simultaneously)

Reauthorization: Required annually for all specialty drugs. Must demonstrate clinical benefit (per validated disease measure) and continued medical necessity.

Step Therapy Appeals: Members or providers may request an exception to step therapy requirements by submitting a clinical exception request demonstrating medical contraindication, previous treatment failure, or urgent clinical need.
