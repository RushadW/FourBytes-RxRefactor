SECTION 1: IMMUNOMODULATORS / BIOLOGICS

1.1 Adalimumab (Humira, Hadlima, Hyrimoz, Cyltezo)

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions
Tier: Specialty Tier 5
Quantity Limit: 2 syringes per 28 days

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. FDA-labeled indication required: RA, PsA, AS, nr-axSpA, plaque psoriasis (BSA ≥10%), CD, UC, HS, uveitis, or juvenile arthritis.
2. Prescriber must be appropriate specialist for the condition being treated.
3. Failed conventional therapy (DMARD for inflammatory arthritis; topical agents for psoriasis; mesalamine/immunomodulators for IBD) UNLESS disease severity precludes delayed treatment.
4. TB screening (IGRA or TST) within 12 months, negative result.
5. Hepatitis B screening before initiation.
6. BCBS Illinois mandates biosimilar adalimumab (Hadlima, Hyrimoz, or Cyltezo) as preferred. Reference product Humira covered only with documented clinical failure of biosimilar.

Step Therapy Required: YES
Step 1: Conventional DMARD (MTX, LEF, HCQ, SSZ) – minimum 12 weeks for RA/PsA.
Step 2: For RA only: one non-TNF biologic (abatacept or tocilizumab preferred) before adalimumab, UNLESS patient has prior TNF inhibitor experience.

Age Restriction: 2+ years
Notes: BCBS Illinois has a TWO-step requirement for RA (more restrictive than Aetna and Cigna, which allow direct TNF access after DMARD failure).

---

1.2 Dupilumab (Dupixent) - UPDATED Q2 2025

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions
Tier: Specialty Tier 4
Quantity Limit: 2 syringes per 28 days

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. Atopic Dermatitis (now approved 6 months+): IGA ≥3 or EASI ≥16. Must have failed two topical therapies. Dermatologist or allergist required.
2. Asthma (12+): Eosinophil ≥150 cells/µL or FeNO ≥25 ppb. Failed ICS/LABA.
3. CRSwNP (18+): Bilateral polyps confirmed by imaging or endoscopy. Failed INCS 4 weeks.
4. EoE (12+): Confirmed eosinophilic esophagitis on biopsy (≥15 eos/HPF). Failed PPI 8 weeks and dietary modifications.
5. Prurigo Nodularis (18+): Confirmed diagnosis by dermatologist. Inadequate response to topical therapy.

Step Therapy Required: YES (all indications except EoE)
Step 1: Topical corticosteroid - 4 weeks minimum.
Step 2: Topical calcineurin inhibitor or crisaborole - 4 weeks.

Age Restriction: 6 months+ (AD - UPDATED from 12 months in Q1); 12+ (asthma, EoE); 18+ (CRSwNP, PN)

Notes: Q2 2025 change - age restriction for atopic dermatitis lowered from 12 months to 6 months, aligning with FDA label update.

---

1.3 Secukinumab (Cosentyx)

Coverage Status: Covered with Restrictions
Tier: Specialty Tier 4

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. FDA-approved indication required.
2. For plaque psoriasis: PASI ≥10 or BSA ≥10% or DLQI >10. Failed 2 topical agents AND phototherapy (or phototherapy contraindicated/refused with documentation).
3. For PsA: failed NSAID minimum 4 weeks AND one conventional DMARD minimum 12 weeks.
4. For AS/nr-axSpA: failed ≥2 NSAIDs at maximum doses for ≥4 weeks each.

Step Therapy Required: YES
Step 1: NSAIDs (4 weeks for AS/PsA); Topical therapy (for psoriasis).
Step 2: Conventional DMARD (for PsA only, minimum 12 weeks).

SECTION 2: METABOLIC AND ENDOCRINE AGENTS

2.1 Semaglutide (Ozempic, Wegovy) - UPDATED Q2 2025

Coverage Status: Covered (Medical Benefit)
Tier: Specialty Tier 3
Quantity Limit: 1 pen per 28 days

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria (Ozempic - Type 2 Diabetes):
1. Confirmed Type 2 diabetes diagnosis with HbA1c ≥7.0%.
2. Q2 2025 UPDATE: Step therapy requirement REMOVED. Semaglutide is now available without requiring prior metformin failure, given cardiovascular outcome data.
3. Prescriber is managing patient's diabetes (any specialty).
4. Document patient does not have personal or family history of MTC or MEN2.

Prior Authorization Criteria (Wegovy - Obesity):
1. BMI ≥30 OR BMI ≥27 with documented obesity-related comorbidity.
2. Participation in structured weight management program.
3. No requirement for prior pharmacotherapy failure (first-line approved).
4. Prescriber attestation that patient has been counseled on dietary and lifestyle modifications.

Step Therapy Required: NO (UPDATED - step therapy for diabetes REMOVED in Q2 2025)
Age Restriction: 12+ (Ozempic); 12+ (Wegovy - now includes adolescents per FDA label)
Notes: SIGNIFICANT CHANGE from Q1 2025. Previous policy required metformin trial for all diabetes patients. Q2 policy removes this requirement.

---

2.2 Tirzepatide (Mounjaro, Zepbound) - NEW in Q2 2025

Coverage Status: Covered (Medical Benefit - newly added)
Tier: Specialty Tier 3

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria (Zepbound - Obesity):
1. BMI ≥30 OR BMI ≥27 with documented comorbidity.
2. No requirement to try semaglutide first (parallel first-line option).
3. Structured weight management program enrollment.
4. Any prescriber who is managing obesity (specialist not required).

Prior Authorization Criteria (Mounjaro - Diabetes):
1. Type 2 diabetes with HbA1c ≥7.0%.
2. No step therapy required.
3. Diabetes management provider.

Step Therapy Required: NO
Age Restriction: 18+ (both indications)
Notes: BCBS Illinois is an early adopter of tirzepatide coverage. No step therapy required for either diabetes or obesity indications.

SECTION 3: ONCOLOGY

3.1 Pembrolizumab (Keytruda)

Coverage Status: Covered (Medical Benefit - IV infusion)
Tier: Specialty Oncology Tier

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. FDA-approved indication with appropriate biomarker testing (PD-L1, MSI, TMB as required by label).
2. Oncologist prescriber required.
3. Performance status documented (ECOG 0-2 preferred; ECOG 3 may be covered with oncologist attestation).
4. No active autoimmune disease or systemic immunosuppression that would preclude safe administration.
5. Organ function criteria (baseline LFTs, creatinine, CBC).

Step Therapy Required: NO (first-line approved for many indications)

---

3.2 Nivolumab (Opdivo)

Coverage Status: Covered (Medical Benefit)
Tier: Specialty Oncology Tier

Prior Authorization Required: YES

Prior Authorization Criteria:
1. FDA-approved indication required.
2. Oncologist prescriber.
3. For combination (nivolumab + ipilimumab): both agents must be authorized simultaneously.

SECTION 4: GENERAL POLICY PROVISIONS

Site of Care Requirements:
- All IV biologic therapies must be administered in an approved outpatient infusion center or hospital outpatient department.
- Home infusion approved only for subcutaneous formulations with documented patient training.
- BCBS Illinois has a Site of Care program: non-urgent IV infusions should be performed at lower-cost outpatient sites when available.

Reauthorization:
- Annual reauthorization required for all specialty biologics.
- Documentation of clinical response (appropriate validated scale improvement) required.
- Failure to demonstrate response may result in non-renewal.

Coverage Exclusions:
- Off-label use without peer-reviewed Level I evidence.
- Cosmetic applications.
- Investigational therapies outside approved clinical trials.
- Concurrent biologic or JAK inhibitor combinations without specific medical evidence.

Policy Change Summary (Q1 2025 → Q2 2025):
1. Semaglutide (diabetes): Step therapy requirement removed.
2. Dupilumab: Minimum age for atopic dermatitis lowered from 12 months to 6 months.
3. Tirzepatide (Mounjaro/Zepbound): Added as new covered drug for both diabetes and obesity.
4. Secukinumab: Added phototherapy requirement for plaque psoriasis (new step).
5. Pembrolizumab: Performance status documentation now required (new criterion).
